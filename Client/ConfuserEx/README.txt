Place ConfuserEx files here to embed them into NytroxRAT.Client.exe

Download from: https://github.com/mkaring/ConfuserEx/releases

Files needed:
  Confuser.CLI.exe
  Confuser.CLI.exe.config
  Confuser.Core.dll
  Confuser.DynCipher.dll
  Confuser.Protections.dll
  Confuser.Renamer.dll
  Confuser.Runtime.dll

Once placed here, rebuild the Client project.
They will be embedded inside NytroxRAT.Client.exe automatically —
no separate files needed when you distribute the Client.
